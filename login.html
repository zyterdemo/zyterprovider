When should members goto an Urgent Care Center(UCC)?

Use a UCC for a non-life threatening condition sucn a:

  • Sore throat, Cough or runny nose
  • Muscle Strains/Sprains
  • Minor Outs/Burns
  • Earache
  • Flashes
